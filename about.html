Northlane started from a simple frustration: too many handoffs between "the design" and "the real thing." We do both, on purpose, under one roof.

How we work

Semantics first

The right HTML element, before any CSS or JavaScript touches it. It's the cheapest accessibility and SEO work you'll ever do.

Show the seams

We'd rather explain a tradeoff than hide it. If a deadline forces a shortcut, you'll know exactly what it cost.

Ship in slices

Small, reviewable pieces of real, working product beat one enormous reveal at the end — for us and for you.

Write it down

Decisions live in a doc, not a Slack thread that scrolls away. Anyone who joins the project later can catch up without asking.
